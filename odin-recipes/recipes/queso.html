Queso

Description

This recipe will show you how to make the cheesiest queso, with a hint of spice. This queso will be perfect for Super Bowl parties paired with delicious crunchy tostitos chips.

Ingredients

  • Chili Powder
  • Evaporated Milk
  • White American Cheese
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Cornstarch
  • Chili Powder

Steps

  1. Heat Evaporated milk and whisk it in cornstarch.
  2. Stir in your cheese until fully melted
  3. Stir in any add-ins,red pepper and chili powder
  4. ENJOY with chips.
