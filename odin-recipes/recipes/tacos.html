Tacos

Description

This recipe will show you how to make the absolute tastiest Tacos.

These Tacos wil not only have a great taste, but will also make sure to provide a great source of carbs and protein and valuable nutrients.

Ingredients

  • Lean Ground Beef
  • Salsa
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Onions
  • Chili Powder
  • Taco Shells

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 250 deegrees Farenheit and place ground beef and onions in a skillet for about 8 to 10 minutes.
  2. Stir in chili powder and onions and reduce heat.
  3. Put meat into a hard shell taco and top it off with lettuce, tomatoes and salsa.
